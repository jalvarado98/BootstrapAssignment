About Me

Although we have better taco joints in my hometown (Laredo, Texas), I have to congratulate this city for the constant events going on and the great amount of job opportunities. I originally moved out here to major in jazz performance at Texas State. After a year there, I concluded that it was not the career path I wanted. I noticed how tech savvy Austin was, and then decided to pursue a career in a vital part of technology.
